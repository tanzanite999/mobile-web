A Website for New Vision

Former president Wang Zerun (2017~2018) built this website in October, 2018. He wanted New Vision Photography Club to be known not only in Yangzhou High School of Jiangsu Province, but also around the globe. A marvelous displaying stage for young phtographers, the HOME page tells them that their works are recognized and valued by New Vision that they are improving their skills and expressing their ideas.

I hope that this website can be passed down forever, a vibrant and peaceful stage for energetic and innovative photographers.

Wang Zerun
